#ChatAE

#ChatAE is a platform that brings together people of various cultures and backgrounds at a single virtual location - anytime, anywhere

 

Enabling real-time, open communication between the vast variety of the residents of the great desert empire, the UAE, #ChatAE does not intend to be "yet another chat service", rather an informative and accessible companion, that not only illuminates the ongoings and festivities of the Emirates, but also tends to present the users with an experience that is robust, yet simple enough to blend in with their daily lives with ease

#ChatAE was designed with the "mobile-first" mantra in mind, giving the members the freedom to access #ChatAE on the go

 

A responsive cross-platform communication solution

An XMPP client that connects to TBD service

A web app that works on all devices that support [X]HTML5

A simple chat application with a consistent, yet powerful experience, as other popular chat apps

Accessible from anywhere, at any time!

"Simplicity is the ultimate form of sophistication"

- Leonardo DaVinci

Our motto is "mobile-first", but our philosophy is simplicity.

Following the footsteps of technology giants, we emphasized on keeping #ChatAE as simple as possible. We are supporters of Modern, Material, and Flat designs, and therefore adhere to the "content before chrome" guidelines: keeping various imaging effects to as little as possible, while presenting our users with precise, to-the-point information, while maintaining the asthetics of #ChatAE

We have used various images and technologies found around the internet. Please note that we have only borrowed them for the sake of this project.
